Trash Belt

10 Feb. 2019 • #game

Fly through space and try not to crash! A game for Android where your goal is to collect coins. I used Unity a lot for prototyping smaller games, but always wanted to try Godot. This game is the result of me playing around with it over the course of a weekend. I made most of this game over the course of a weekend and added more features over time.
